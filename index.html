Snow Scotland
I had an itch to scratch. I wanted to aggregate all the latest information on Scottish snow conditions. Snow Scotland was the result of this. Its specifically designed with low bandwidth conditions in mind for days when you're out in the hills already and need up-to-date information.

European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control
ECDC were looking for a way to improve the availability and accessibility of their data. I spent several months working with a group from ECDC to build The Surveillance Atlas of Infectious Diseases. The work involved integrating InstantAtlas with backend GIS and REST API services hosted by ECDC. The final handover involved training the developers at ECDC to take over the running of the system.
InstantAtlas
InstantAtlas is a set of tools for designing, populating and publishing Dynamic Reports to the web. One of my main responsibilities was creating the front end templates that our clients used to generate their own reports. Not only did the templates have to look good, but they also had to be capable of being customised, styled and designed by non-technical people.
