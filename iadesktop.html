InstantAtlas Desktop
InstantAtlas Desktop is a set of tools for designing, populating and publishing dashboards to the web. I worked on the dashboards that our customers used to visualize their data. I was solely responsible for the design and development of the functionality, mapping, charting and components contained in the dashboards. Initially developed using Flex and ActionScript the dashboards were subsequently rebuilt from the ground up for modern browsers and devices using JavaScript, Jquery, HTML5 Canvas and SVG.
